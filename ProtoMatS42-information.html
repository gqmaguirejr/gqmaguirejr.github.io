LPKF ProtoMat S42 information

This page contains some information for students using the LPKF ProtoMat S42 in the laboratory of Professors Smith and Maguire.

Tools

Cutters and routers

The usual cutter is a Universal cutter (Universalfräs) with a cutting diameter (räsdiamater) of 0.2 - 0.5 mm

The main contour router (Konturfräs) has a cutting diameter (Fräsdiameter of 1.60 mm

Drills(Spiralborr)

Drilling dimater (Borrdiameter): 0.6mm, 0.8mm, and 0.9mm

Board stock

Double sided FR4 sheets 229 mm x 305 mm (9 inch x 12 inch) with 35/35 µm copper in the special format for the ProtoMat S-series (i.e., with pre-drilled (förborrade) 3 mm holes.

There is also some single sided board stock.
